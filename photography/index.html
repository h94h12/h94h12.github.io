Photography

Photography is a personal hobby of mine that I take quite seriously. I haven't taken any proper photo classes and learn mostly from experience. I like to focus on what emotion the resulting photo conveys, instead of obsessing if a photo is "technically correct".

Perhaps the goal of my photography is not to dazzle and amaze, but rather to make one moment of existence unforgettable and worthy of unbearable nostalgia.

I'd rather have photos with a characteristic mood to them instead of just the model smiling. I aim to capture a certain fleeting feeling that can't quite be described in words.

Feel free to message me if you are interested in modeling or are a fellow photographer and you'd like to do a collaboration. I'd love to meet people with similar interests from the area!
